Methodology: Vibe scores are weekly foot-traffic forecasts (0–100% relative to each venue's own peak) sourced from BestTime.app. The badge for each venue reflects its current intensity for the present Nairobi day-of-week and hour. The chart inside each card is today's full 24-hour curve.

Coverage: Limited to Nairobi venues with sufficient public foot-traffic data. Many iconic spots aren't yet indexed — working on it.
